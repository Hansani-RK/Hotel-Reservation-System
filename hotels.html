Swimming Pool..

We have three swimming pools at Heritance Classic.

Kaludiya Pool

Kaludiya means “black water” – the bottom of the pool is the dark bedrock on which the structure of the hotel rests. The surface of the rock has been coated with plastic to prevent water seepage and make it easier to clean the pool.
This natural pool is another example of Geoffrey Bawa’s ingenious blending of the natural topography with the architectural features of the hotel.

    Open: 8am – 8pm daily Depth: 1.3 metres – 1.7 metres
